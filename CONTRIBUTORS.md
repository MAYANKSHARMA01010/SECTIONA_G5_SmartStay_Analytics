## 🎯 Our Mission
Our goal was simple: **Turn raw Airbnb data into actionable investment intelligence.** 

From cleaning the first row of messy CSV data to calculating advanced business metrics like RevPAR and Investment Potential, every member of this team played a crucial role in bringing the **SmartStay** vision to life.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
*   **Python:** The engine for data extraction and cleaning.
*   **Pandas & NumPy:** For the heavy lifting and math.
*   **Matplotlib & Seaborn:** For our initial visual "sanity checks."
*   **Tableau:** The final stage where we bring the story to life.
