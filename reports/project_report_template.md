# SmartStay Analytics: Project Report

## 1. Introduction
- **Problem Statement:** How can we optimize hotel placement and pricing in Albany using Airbnb data?
- **Objectives:** Maximize ROI for investors, understand guest preferences, and track platform growth.

## 2. Data Extraction & Cleaning
- **Source:** Inside Airbnb (Feb 2026).
- **Processing:** 3-pass cleaning pipeline (metadata removal, median imputation, currency normalization).
- **Final Dataset:** 32,585 listings across 36 features.

## 3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- **Price Distribution:** Insights into budget vs luxury market segments.
- **Neighborhood Performance:** Top revenue-generating areas in Albany.
- **Room Type Mix:** Dominance of Entire Home listings.

## 4. Statistical Analysis
- **Hypothesis 1:** Impact of Superhost status on pricing.
- **Hypothesis 2:** Revenue variance across property types.
- **Correlation:** Primary drivers of listing occupancy.

## 5. Predictive Modeling (Regression)
- **Simple Linear Regression:** Nightly price vs. Accommodates.
- **Multiple Linear Regression:** Predicting revenue based on 12 key features.

## 6. Business Recommendations
- **Investment Strategy:** Recommended neighborhoods for new listings.
- **Pricing Strategy:** Dynamic pricing factors.
- **Quality Control:** Essential amenities for high ratings.

## 7. Conclusion
- Summary of findings and future scope (e.g., sentiment analysis of reviews).
